About me

A small introduction about myself

Ganan Sivagnanenthirarajah

Electrical Engineering Student

My pursuit to become an electrical engineer stems from my experiences and academics. In my senior years of high school, I led a group of 40+ students as the president and founder of a rookie FIRST Robotics Team (FRC 6632). Through this experience and my academic interests in physics, mathematics and computer science, I decided to pursue Electrical Engineering at the University of Waterloo.

I completed my first work term at the City of Toronto, Water Division on the Process Control Systems Networking team, and gained knowledge in web development, prototyping and network maintenance. In my second work term at Nielsen, I programmed several applications to increase efficiency using OpenCV (Computer Vision), Tesseract OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and Selenium (web automation tool). In my third work term at Carfax, I developed a strong interest in iOS development creating a prototype using several different frameworks and structures. In my fourth internship, I made the perfect pivot into technical product management at Lifion by ADP on the Platform Interfaces team. Finally, in my most recent work term, I returned to Lifion by ADP as a technical product manager on the Mobile team. Currently, I am studying in my first term of my fourth year (4A).
