A small introduction about myself

Ganan Sivagnanenthirarajah

Technical Product Manager | Electrical Engineer

My pursuit to become a Technical Product Manager stems from my experiences and academics. In my senior years of high school, I led a group of 40+ students as the president and founder of a rookie FIRST Robotics Team (FRC 6632). Through this experience and my academic interests in physics, mathematics and computer science, I decided to pursue Electrical Engineering at the University of Waterloo. Through my internships I found my passion for building solutions and products in software.

In my cumulative 3 years of employment in software development, I have proven to be an innovative leader. I am recognized for creatively architecting and launching Lifion's Unified Messaging System to scale user notifications and significantly improve client sentiment scores.
